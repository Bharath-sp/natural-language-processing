== Introduction ==
Once the corpus is created, modifications are required to make the text suitable for processing. Preprocessing steps are unique to a problem. Understanding the problem is very crucial in choosing preprocessing steps. Improper processing schemes may lead to loss of lexical context. Preprocessing consists of

. Tokenization (Splitting the text into words)
. Normalization (normalizing seconds, sec, s to a standard notation)
. Substitution (substituting seconds, sec, s with a standard notation)

In all these, we typically do:

* Case folding - Convert all text into lower case
* Stemming - running -> run
* Lemmatization - best -> good
* Remove misspellings
* Remove punctuations
* Remove white space, newline, tabs
* Remove contradictions - isn't -> is not, I'd -> I would
* Remove scripts, form variables for HTML and XML.
